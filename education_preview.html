What is the business judgment rule and how does it protect board members? ‚ñæ
The business judgment rule is a legal standard that shields board members from personal liability for decisions made in good faith, with reasonable care, and in the best interest of the building. As long as you can demonstrate that the board followed a reasonable process ‚Äî getting competitive bids, consulting experts, documenting decisions ‚Äî courts will generally not second-guess the outcome. Should my building hire a managing agent or self-manage? ‚ñæ
Most buildings with 30+ units benefit from professional management. A good managing agent handles day-to-day operations, vendor coordination, compliance filings, and financial reporting. The key is accountability ‚Äî BoardIQ gives your board visibility into costs, deadlines, and vendor performance so you can hold your managing agent to measurable standards.
